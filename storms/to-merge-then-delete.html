Billion Dollar Storms

35 out of 780 storms exceeded a billion dollars in damage from 2013 to 2018. These 35 storms accounted for 80% of direct storm fatalities, which totalled 14,495 out of 18,111. Stats do not yet include the end of 2018 (Florence and Michael). Actual costs are significantly higher than WikiPedia reporting. Check Weather Underground U.S. Hurricane Damage Totals.

The next time you're traveling in response to a severe hurricane or typhoon, turn on your Twitter geoposition and tweet away. You'll likely end up in our data analysis of mobility patterns that occur before, during and after billion dollar storms. View One Million Real-Time Tweets
