Tweet Storms

We're chasing storms, with Twitter. The next time you're traveling in response to a severe hurricane or typhoon, turn on your Twitter geoposition and tweet away. You'll likely end up in our data analysis of mobility patterns that occur before, during and after billion dollar storms.

35 out of 780 storms exceeded a billion dollars in damage from 2013 to Sept 22, 2018.
These 35 storms accounted for 80% of direct storm fatalities, which totalled 14,495 out of 18,111.

These 35 "billion dollar storms" caused 389.5 billion in damage (US dollars) during the last 6 years, averaging 65 billion per year - a significant increase from the prior average of 10 billion in damage per year.
