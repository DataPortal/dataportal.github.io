Tweet Storms

We're chasing storms, with Twitter. The next time you're traveling to avoid a severe hurricane or typhoon, turn on your Twitter geoposition and Tweet away. You'll likely end up in our data analysis of mobility patterns that occur before, during and after billion dollar storms.

31 out of 549 cyclones exceeded a billion dollars in damage from 2013 to Sept 22, 2018. (Includes hurricanes, typhoons and tropical depressions.)
These 31 storms accounted for 80.8% of deaths caused by all cyclones during the same period (14,467 of 17,901).
